PIPELINE

We are developing medicines to selectively eliminate senescent cells, called senolytic medicines. Derma topical treatment is our initial focus on delivering localized therapy in xeroderma pigmentosum, cockayne syndrome, and skin cancer. Beyond cellular senescence, we are discovering additional ways of intervening age-associated diseases.

Fisetin + Nanoparticles

Creating the first clinical treatment targeting aging for accelerated aging diseases

24-hour Derma Monitoring, Anywhere, Anytime

Harish Chandra Pal et al., 2014 PLos One
Gun-Dong Kim et al., 2014 Food and Chem Toxicol
Myung-Soo Shon et al., 2016 Food Sci Biotechnol

Fisetin has been shown to treat many aging-related skin conditions and diseases in preclinical trials.

Fisetin is not very soluble and is a weak binder to its target. On its own, fisetin cannot be delivered across the skin.

Modified nanoparticles can help solubilize fisetin.

Embedded nanoparticles also help deliver fisetin across the barrier of the skin for penetrating effects underneath the skin.
