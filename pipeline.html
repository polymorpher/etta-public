PIPELINE

We are first targeting psoriasis as a proof-of-principle of our approach, then rapidly expanding to aging-related diseases with unmet needs.

Start with the Skin

Fisetin has been shown to treat multiple skin conditions in mice, including dermatitis, UV damage, xeroderma pigmentosum and melanoma, so we will first use our fisetin nanoparticles to target the skin. By starting with the skin, we can move quickly and safely into the clinic to demonstrate the benefits of our approach. The skin condition psoriasis shares many problems with aging, including senescence, insulin resistance, and inflammation, which can be relieved by fisetin. We hope to treat these patients as a proof-of-principle that fisetin can address these problems in the clinic.
