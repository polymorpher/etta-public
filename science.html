AGING THERAPEUTICS


Senescent cell removal, insulin signaling inhibition/caloric restriction, and rapamycin (mTOR inhibition) extend lifespan in mice the most in our meta-analysis of over 100 different aging interventions. Surprisingly, the natural molecule fisetin can do all three, offering great promise as a therapeutic.
