Approaching COVID-19 via Aging Interventions

COVID Deaths

Aging treatments reset lung function, circulatory function, and inflammation to a youthful state, leading to significantly reduced COVID-19 deaths.

Vaccine Immune System

As COVID-19 cases rise, the vaccine is likely not ready until 2021. Aging therapies can treat people at risk before hospitalization by boosting their defenses. During COVID-19 and before future pandemics, aging treatments can also heighten vaccine response.

Early Detection Target Treatment

Early detection and target treatment are needed for post-COVID syndromes, such as COVID-19 induced pulmonary fibrosis. Magnetic biosensors and nanoparticles can also be used to support biopharmaceutical development with high sensitivity and high specificity at lost cost.

Post-COVID Syndromes

Aging increases the probability of permanent organ damage and causes brain fog and chronic fatigue syndrome in older individuals. Aging interventions increase the cellular recovery power for post-COVID syndromes.
